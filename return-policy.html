Return Policy

Simple and hassle-free returns for your complete satisfaction

Customer Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind our products and services. If you're not completely satisfied, we make returns easy and convenient.

30-Day Returns

Return items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange

Original Condition

Items must be unused, undamaged, and in original packaging with all accessories

Proof Required

Original receipt, order confirmation, or invoice must be provided

Free Returns

We cover return shipping costs for defective or incorrect items

How to Return Your Item

1
Contact Our Support Team

Email us at returns@arsha.com or call +1 5589 55488 55 to initiate your return request

2
Receive Return Authorization

We'll send you a Return Authorization Number (RAN) and detailed return instructions within 24 hours

3
Package and Ship

Pack the item securely with the provided return label and RAN. Drop it off at any authorized shipping location

4
Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and process your return, expect your refund within 5-7 business days
