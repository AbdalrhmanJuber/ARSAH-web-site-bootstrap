Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last Updated: September 1, 2025
Your Privacy Matters

At ARSHA, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, make a purchase, or contact us.

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Payment and billing information
  • Usage data and website analytics
  • Preferences and settings
How We Use Your Information
Service Delivery
  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process transactions and payments
  • Send service-related communications
Improvement & Analytics
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve our services and features
  • Develop new products and services
Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

Required for basic website functionality, security, and user authentication.
Help us understand how visitors interact with our website to improve user experience.
Used to deliver personalized advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption
SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
Secure Storage
Protected servers and databases
